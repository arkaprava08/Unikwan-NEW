User experience design is a process in design which enables building of digital software products and services.

The idea of UX is derived from User centered design. What user wants and what are the goals and how they want to achieve them?

Aligning business requirement to user requirement is the key to good user experience.

What we do and how we do?

We follow the process by involving all the stakeholders and gather insights to design a brief.

  • Kick-off meeting/ Workshop
  • Business Requirement gathering and analysis
  • User requirement gathering and analysis
  • Workshop: Derive patterns and categorise content based on finding and above steps
  • Information Architecture
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • Visual Design

Based on the requirement our team decides to carve a process which fits well to intended product.
